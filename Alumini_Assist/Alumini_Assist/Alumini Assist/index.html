The Center for Alumni Connect at Shoolini University was established in 2021. Its primary goal is to engage alumni in various activities to strengthen the university’s community and further its development. The first batch of BBA, BSc, Post Graduate Diploma in Data Science, and Post Graduate Diploma in Dietetics graduated in June 2020, with a total of 171 students.
The center recognizes that alumni are vital to the university’s growth, as they serve as brand ambassadors who can help with networking, placement activities, and even attracting new students for admissions. Their involvement in these areas can play a significant role in the university's success.
