Welcome

As a proud Shoolini University alumnus, you are an integral part of our vibrant and growing community. Our Alumni Portal is designed to keep you connected to your alma mater, fellow graduates, and current students. Whether you're looking to reconnect with old friends, explore career opportunities, or give back to the university that helped shape your future, this portal serves as a platform for all that and more.

Our Purpose

The Shoolini University Alumni Portal was created to strengthen the bonds between our alumni and the university, ensuring a continued exchange of knowledge, experience, and opportunities. We aim to provide our alumni with the tools they need to stay engaged with the university, stay updated on important news, and contribute to the growth of future generations of students.

Why Join the Alumni Portal?

  • Reconnect and Network: Stay in touch with classmates, faculty, and peers
  • Access Resources: Find career advice and opportunities
  • Stay Informed: Receive updates and event invitations
  • Give Back: Support future generations
  • Mentor Students: Share your expertise
