Volunteer

Join us as a volunteer for AMP and make a meaningful impact! By sharing your time and skills, you can contribute to exciting projects, gain valuable experience, and connect with a passionate community. Volunteering is a chance to grow, learn, and make a difference. Whether you have a few hours or more, your efforts will help drive positive change. We welcome everyone willing to lend a helping hand. Sign up today and be a part of something bigger—your contributions matter!

What is the Alumni Mentorship Program?

This program connects alumni with students to guide, inspire, empower and network with them for a brighter future.
AMP will provide you:

  • One-on-One Mentorship: Personalized guidance for career and academics.
  • Career Insights: Real-world knowledge about industries and roles.
  • Networking: Build connections with professionals and peers.
  • Skill Development: Enhance soft and technical skills.
  • Resume Support: Help with resumes and interview prep.
